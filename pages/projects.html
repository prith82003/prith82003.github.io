Solar System

From a young age, I've been captivated by the vast expanse of space, marveling at its grand scale and the intricate physics governing celestial interactions. Motivated by Sebastian Lague's video, I embarked on crafting my own simulation of gravitational forces. Employing Newton's Gravitational Equation, I fashioned a simulation that vividly illustrates the cosmic ballet performed by celestial bodies.
